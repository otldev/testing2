Tribe

Similar to 'Nation', however this term has a specific meaning derived from non-Indigenous culture, so may not be suitable for use with the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander community.

Mob

A term used to identify a group of Aboriginal people associated with a particular place or country.

Clan

A clan is a local descent group, larger than a family but based on family links through a common ancestry.

Sorry business

This is the cultural and social obligation of family, friends and associates to take part in an extended period of mourning following the death of an Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander person.

Stolen generation

The Stolen Generation describes how many Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders children were stolen and removed from their communities and families as children by Australian Federal and State government agencies and church missions under
acts of parliaments.

Country

A culturally defined area of land associated with a distinct group of people or nation.

Traditional owner/custodian

An Aboriginal
and Torres Strait Islander person or persons directly descended from the original Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander inhabitants of a culturally defined area
of land or country.

Elder

An Elder is a highly respected person in the Aboriginal or Torres Strait Island community who possesses specific cultural knowledge and skills. Elders may not always be of advanced age.

Nation

A culturally distinct group associated with a particular culturally defined area of land or country.
